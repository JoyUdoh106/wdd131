Nigeria

Nigeria is situated in West Africa, bordered by Niger to the north, Chad to the northeast, Cameroon to the east, and Benin to the west. Home to 250 ethic groups. Land is approximately 923,768 square kilometers (356,669 square miles). The most populated country with over 200 million people living within. 500 indigenous spoken languages and English as a general language. Approximately 50% of the population lives in urban areas. 60% of Nigerians are within the age oof 25. a country well known for Agriculture which major in the production 0f cassava, yams, maize, and rice. As well as music and art such as fuji, afrobeats and highlife. Home to thriving film industry, known as Nollywood. Nigeria has a rich history with civilization. It was a British colony from 1914 - 1960 before it gained independence. The country currently faces serious security problems, corruption and infrastructure
