Website Planning Document

Site Name

SmartPet Connect

This name represents a web platform for smart pet care tracking and guidance, integrating tools for monitoring pet health, feeding, and exercise. It's relevant for tech-savvy pet owners who want smarter ways to take care of their pets.

Optional domain availability: smartpetconnect.com

Site Purpose

SmartPet Connect helps pet owners track and improve their pets’ well-being using smart tools. The site offers pet care tips, smart device recommendations, and a way to track feeding, medication, and activity history online.

Scenarios

  • How can I monitor my pet's diet and feeding times?
  • What smart collars are best for tracking my dog's activity?

Color Schema

Primary color: #2a4d69 (used for headers and accents)

Secondary color: #63ace5 (used for buttons, links, and icons)

Typography

  • Roboto – Used for all body text for readability
  • Montserrat – Used for headings to establish visual hierarchy
